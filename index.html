Our Story

Meet Nomsa

Nomsa discovered her love for nail artistry at 22, practising on friends and family until her apartment became everyone's favourite beauty destination. What started as passion quickly became a calling.

After completing her professional nail technology qualification, Nomsa founded Nails by Nomsa with one simple belief: every woman in South Africa — whether she's a corporate executive or a student — deserves beautiful, well-crafted nails at a price she can afford. Today, that belief still drives every appointment.

🎓

Certified Professional

Fully qualified nail technician with continuous training in the latest techniques.

🧼

Hygiene First

Sterilised tools, clean towels and fresh supplies for every single client.

🌿

Quality Materials

Only trusted, professional-grade products — nothing cheap touches your nails.

🤝

Community-Centred

Proudly South African, supporting local women and building beautiful confidence.
